Welcome to Hunt the Wumpus! The goal is to find the treasure, and escape. The Wumpus is sleeping, but if you enter his room, he will awaken. Use the text hints to find your way around, and make sure to listen for the Wumpus snoring. The circle in the Wump-O-Meter will get larger the closer you are to the Wumpus. Please click the 'Play Sound' for this feature to work. You are the green circle. Navigate through the cave using 'W', 'A', 'S', and 'D'. Press 'Restart Game' to play again and generate a new map.
