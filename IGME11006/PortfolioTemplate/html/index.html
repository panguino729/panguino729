Kat

Katarina Tretter grew up an avid video game lover in Allentown, Pennsylvania. She graduated from Rochester Instituted after 4 years with a Bachelor’s degree in Game Design and Development. Katarina then continued at Rochester Institute of Technology to earn her Master’s in Game Design and Develop at Rochester Institute of Technology. In 2022, Katarina won Best Student Game at the Independent Games Festival for her side-scrolling platformer for PC called Cainsura. She also wrote a book documenting her experiences as a video game developer at Square Enix Tokyo. Katarina is currently living in Allentown, Pennsylvania playing the games she loves.
