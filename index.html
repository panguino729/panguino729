Color-IT

Made using Unity (2021.2.3f1) by Angry Moths (Pruthviraj Solanki, Alex Parrotto, Parth Rustagi, Tom Choi, Katarina Tretter)

In this 2D puzzle game, experience the difficulties of bad color blindness accessibility while trying to submit an assignment. Swap through different kinds of color blindness to solve puzzles, navigate the site, and turn your assignment in on time.

Responsibilities: Created color palette for puzzles, created beginning lab art, designed some windows, created layouts for the second half of the game

The Trial - Choices

Made using Inform7 (1.68.1)

The Trial: Choices starts at the entrance to The Trial. You must survive the various traps and encounters while traversing through the maze. Along the way, you can find documents and clues that hint at the lost history of Stemvia. By gathering all of the lost information, you will have to make a choice at the end of the maze. You will have to choose between leaving and joining society, or staying and joining the Resistance. Each decision will have consequences, so you must choose carefully.
